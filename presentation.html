Premise

CIUFFOLETTI GAMES IS HIRING! We are looking for developers & teams to create game ideas for 4th and 5th grade students!
Your idea should be:

  • School and age appropriate
  • Interesting
  • Creative
  • and a YOU / YOUR TEAM original

We are seeking well-rounded presentations that incorporate music, artwork, and an interesting story!

Your presentation should include:

  • Story behind your game/ characters (the answers to the questions in "Resources")
  • Music demonstration
  • Artwork presentation/ demonstration
  • Any extra-bonus options

While we are open to exploring all game options, we are looking for games that:

  • Teach a concept (Math, Reading, Science, etc)
  • Immerse the player in an interesting story (like an RPG style game)
  • Have an AWESOME music score / soundtrack
