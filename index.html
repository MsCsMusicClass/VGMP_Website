Video Game Music Unit


In the Video Game Music Unit, we have explored computer-generated sound, chiptunes, sound waves, as well as the relation between video game music and movie scores, sound-design, and musical form. Now, students have the opportunity to create their own idea for a video game, including a plot/ story, visual representation, and, of course, music that includes a bassline, percussion accompaniment, and theme and variation or another approved form! ALL STUDENTS will be responsible for contributing their own music and artwork. Students may work alone or they may collaborate with one other person or with two other people.
